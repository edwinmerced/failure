Failure is an option

Failure is commonly known as lack of success, but any succesful entreprenuer will acknowledge the importance of failing. There are always postive lessons that can be learned from business ventures that have failed. Learning from one's failures, as well as the failures of others, can provide valuable insights for success.



The greatest artists like Dylan, Picasso and Newton risked failure. And if we want to be great we've got to risk it too.

Failure is an option

The six stories reported on this page are accounts of entrepreneurs that failed in their business ventures. They are meant to describe and not to blame nor scorn anyone, but to highlight and reflect on the importance of failure and to rescue the value of not being succcesful.

While you explore each story ask yourself: What can I learn from this? What would I have done differently? What would I have done next?
